### 🎯 Business Question

Which investors have participated in the highest number of startup funding rounds?

Understanding investor activity helps founders identify potential funding partners and provides insights into the venture capital landscape.

---

### 📊 Analysis

The analysis counts the total number of funding rounds in which each investor participated, including investments across multiple startups.

---

### 🔍 Key Findings

- **India Quotient** emerged as the most active investor in terms of funding participation.
- Other leading investors such as **Accel Partners**, **Kalaari Capital**, **Sequoia Capital**, and **Blume Ventures** (based on your results) also demonstrated strong investment activity.
- A relatively small group of venture capital firms participated in a significant share of startup funding rounds.

---

### 💼 Business Insight

Highly active investors often have:

- Extensive startup portfolios
- Strong industry networks
- Experience mentoring early-stage companies
- Greater visibility into emerging market trends

Their continued participation highlights confidence in India's growing startup ecosystem.

---

### 🚀 Business Recommendation

Founders preparing to raise capital should prioritize investors whose investment history aligns with their startup stage and industry. Analyzing historical funding patterns can help identify investors who are both active and strategically relevant.

---

### 📈 Why This Matters

Understanding investor activity enables:

- Better fundraising strategies
- Targeted investor outreach
- Stronger market research
- Identification of influential venture capital firms

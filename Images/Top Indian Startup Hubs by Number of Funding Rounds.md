📊 Business Question

Where should a startup establish its headquarters to maximize its chances of securing funding?

🔍 Key Findings

• Bangalore led all cities with the highest number of funded startups.
• Mumbai and New Delhi followed closely.
• Funding activity is concentrated in a few major metropolitan areas.

💼 Business Insight

Investment networks and startup ecosystems remain geographically concentrated, making location an important strategic decision for founders.

🚀 Recommendation

Founders seeking access to venture capital should prioritize established startup ecosystems while evaluating industry-specific advantages offered by each city.

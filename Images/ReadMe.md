### Key Findings
- **Bangalore** leads India's startup ecosystem with the highest number of funded startups.
- **Mumbai** and **New Delhi** follow as major investment hubs.
- The majority of startup activity is concentrated in a handful of metropolitan regions.

### Business Impact
The concentration of funding in these cities highlights the importance of mature startup ecosystems, investor networks, and access to skilled talent. Organizations expanding into India's startup landscape may prioritize these regions for partnerships, investments, and market entry.
